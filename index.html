About Our Villa

Welcome to Mountain Bliss VS Villa, a private hillfront retreat nestled along Maldevta Road, where luxury meets the calm of the mountains. Designed for those who seek a peaceful escape, the villa offers a perfect blend of modern comfort, scenic beauty, and complete privacy.

This elegant 2BHK villa features two spacious bedrooms with attached washrooms, a thoughtfully designed living area, and a fully equipped kitchen. Step outside into your private garden and soak in uninterrupted mountain views, creating the perfect setting to unwind and reconnect.

Whether you're travelling as a couple, with family, or a group of friends, Mountain Bliss VS Villa offers an experience that feels both intimate and indulgent. Enjoy cozy movie nights with projector screens, relaxed outdoor moments, or simply the serenity of nature around you.

With a dedicated caretaker available to assist with meals and your stay, every detail is taken care of—so you can focus on what truly matters: slowing down, breathing in the fresh mountain air, and creating unforgettable memories.
