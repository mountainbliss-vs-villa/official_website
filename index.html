About Our Villa

Welcome to Mountain Bliss VS Villa, a private hillfront retreat nestled along Maldevta Road, where luxury meets the calm of the mountains. Designed for those who seek a peaceful escape, the villa offers a perfect blend of modern comfort, scenic beauty, and complete privacy.

This elegant 2BHK villa features two spacious bedrooms with attached washrooms, a living area, and a fully equipped kitchen. Enjoy movie nights with projector screens and fresh mountain air in your private garden.
